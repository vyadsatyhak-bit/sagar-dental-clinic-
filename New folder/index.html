About the Clinic

A Clinic Built on Trust

Sagar Dental Clinic & Implant Centre has been serving the people of Hoshangabad and Narmadapuram with honest, high-quality dental care. We combine modern equipment with a warm, unhurried approach — because great dental care is as much about how you feel as what we do.

Our lead dental surgeon brings years of hands-on experience in implantology, orthodontics, and cosmetic procedures, ensuring every patient receives personalised treatment designed for their unique needs.

  • ✔ Digital X-rays & modern diagnostics
  • ✔ Painless anaesthesia techniques
  • ✔ Sterilised instruments for every patient
